      GameGuru MAX Limitations

      Not all generated ideas are designed to work within GameGuru MAX's capabilities. Keep in mind these limitations:

      Not Supported:
      • Third-person camera
      • Multiplayer functionality
      • Racing mechanics
      • Complex physics simulations
      Well Supported:
      • First-person exploration
      • Puzzle mechanics
      • Horror/atmospheric games
      • RPG elements
