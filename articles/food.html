Key Properties

  • PROMPT_TEXT: Text displayed when using the food item
  • PROMPT_IF_COLLECTABLE: Text displayed when collecting the food item
  • USEAGE_TEXT: Text displayed when the food is consumed
  • QUANTITY: Amount of health to add or deduct when consuming the food
  • PICKUP_RANGE: Distance at which the player can interact with the food
